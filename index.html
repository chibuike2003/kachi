About Kachi Care Solutions

Kachi Care Solutions is a healthcare support organization dedicated to improving lives through professional caregiving services, healthcare assistant training, and community health initiatives.

We provide compassionate home care services for individuals and families while also training aspiring healthcare assistants with the knowledge and practical skills needed to succeed in healthcare environments.

Our Services

Personal Care Assistance

Support with daily hygiene and personal care such as bathing, grooming, dressing, and toileting while preserving dignity and comfort.

Mobility Support

Assistance with walking, transferring, and repositioning to help prevent falls and improve comfort.

Medication Reminder

Caregivers assist clients in remembering their medication schedules as prescribed by healthcare professionals.

Post Hospital Recovery Care

Support for patients recovering from surgery, illness, or hospital discharge to ensure comfort and safety at home.

Elderly Care and Companionship

Compassionate support and companionship for elderly individuals, helping them maintain emotional wellbeing and independence.

Basic Health Monitoring

Routine observation of vital signs and overall health status, with communication to families when necessary.

Nanny Services for Working Families

Reliable childcare support for working class families who need trusted caregivers to assist with their children.

Special Needs Care With disability

Professional support for individuals living with developmental or physical disabilities.

Care for Special Needs Children

Compassionate and attentive care designed to support children with special care requirements.

Palliative Care Support

Comfort focused care for individuals with serious or life limiting illnesses, ensuring dignity, compassion, and emotional support.

Healthcare Assistant and Caregiver Training Program

Start Your Career in Healthcare

The Kachi Care Solutions Healthcare Assistant and Caregiver Training Program prepares individuals to become competent healthcare support professionals.

Program Duration

3 Months

Total Training Hours

150 Hours

Format

Classroom • Practical • Hands-on • Mentorship

What You Will Learn

  • Basic nursing care & vital signs monitoring
  • Patient hygiene, mobility & infection control
  • Communication, ethics & patient dignity
  • Safeguarding vulnerable adults & children
  • Elderly care, dementia & palliative principles
  • CPR, Basic Life Support & First Aid
  • Nutrition support & safe feeding techniques
  • Emotional support & patient comfort

Founder & Director

Onyekachi Emeribe

Seasoned Nurse with over 15 years of experience in clinical practice and healthcare leadership.
